Who is the Queen of Rap?

Summary

I found a tutorial that shows how to mine tweets and get sentiment values from them. Sentiment is on a scale between 1 and -1. Positive numbers imply positive feelings about the subject, and negative values inply negative feelings about the subject. I decided that it would be interesting to determine who is the queen of rap with twitter sentiment analysis. I collected tweets that tagged Cardi B (@iamcardib), Megan thee Stallion (@theestallion), and Nicki Minaj (@NICKIMINAJ).

Code stored at Github.

Results

I collected 4808 tweets in the span of 24 hours about the three rappers total. As shown in figure 1, Nicki Minaj had the most tweets at 3692. Both Cardi and Megan had approximately 560 tweets each. By a landslide, Nicki has a bigger precense on twitter than the other two rappers.

Figure 1

To see the distribution of tweets for each rapper, I used histograms as shown in figure 2. It is interesting to note that most tweets are regarded as neutral (sentiment score of 0). Although Nicki had the most tweets, the majority of those tweets were neutral. Megan's tweets were also mostly neutral, which is surprising since she was running a sweepstakes where she sent money to users through the app Cash App. Also I believe that the TextBlob library does not recognize slang terms which could change the sentiment of tweets to be more positive or negative. The majority of Cardi's tweets were in the positive range. This was because of a trending tag: #GaanaUsersChoiceIcons. Users replied to a tweet with their choice of best female voice. Since Cardi was a choice in that tweet and the TextBlob library scored its sentiment as positive, most of Cardi's tweets were in the positive range.

Figure 2

As predicted by the histograms, Cardi won the highest average sentiment score (Figure 3). Since the majority of her positive scores came from the #GaanaUsersChoiceIcons tweet, I do not think her average sentiment score is very accurate. To accurately crown a queen of rap, we would need a sentiment analyzer that can accurately measure sentiment with slang. Since Nicki had the most tweets overall, we can crown her the queen.
